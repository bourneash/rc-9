Remote Command — Free Online Artillery Tank Game

Remote Command is a browser-based artillery tank game in the tradition of Scorched Earth and the classic DOS-era artillery genre. Aim with angle and power, account for wind, and blast craters out of destructible terrain. No download, no install — playable directly in any modern browser.

Game modes

  • Classic free-for-all: last tank standing wins.
  • Teams (2 × 4): two teams of up to four tanks each.
  • Solo target practice: single-player score attack against pop-up targets.
  • Realtime mode: no turns — fire continuously with reload cooldowns.

Weapons and environments

Twenty-plus weapons including missile, heavy shell, nuke, MIRV, cluster bomb, bunker buster, drill, laser, napalm, acid, EMP, land mine, bouncing bomb, supply crate, shield, and a full suite of air-strike markers. Play across ten environments — forest, desert, canyon, arctic, ocean (with full underwater physics), cave, moon, Mars, futuristic, and randomised mixes — with day, dusk, and night cycles.

Controls

Use the arrow keys for angle and power, Space or Enter to fire, and the on-screen joystick or touch dial on mobile. See the how-to-play guide for the full controls and weapon reference.
