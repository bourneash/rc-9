Privacy policy

Remote Command is a free browser game. We collect limited data to understand how the site is used. No accounts, no purchases, no personal data required to play.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to measure sessions and player engagement (pages visited, time on site). GA4 is configured with IP anonymization. You can opt out via the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

We also use Cloudflare Web Analytics for aggregate traffic counts. This is cookieless and collects no personal information.

Cookies

Google Analytics sets first-party cookies (_ga, _ga_*) to distinguish unique sessions. These contain no personal information and expire after 2 years and 1 day. The Cloudflare beacon sets no cookies. No other cookies are set by this site.

What we don't collect

We do not collect your name, email, location, or any account information. Game state is stored locally in your browser and never sent to any server. We do not run ads, sell data, or use retargeting pixels.

Third parties

Analytics data is processed by Google and Cloudflare under their respective privacy policies. We have no other third-party data relationships.
