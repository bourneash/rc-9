## Medium Description (500 chars)
Remote Command is a modern browser reboot of the legendary Scorched Earth tank-artillery genre. Pick your angle, dial in your power, account for wind — and launch missiles, nukes, MIRVs, torpedoes, homing shells, lasers, and 15+ more weapons across fully destructible terrain. Battle smart AI opponents across 9 themed environments from forest to ocean to Mars. Mobile-optimized with touch controls, 60fps physics, and turn-based tactics. Free to play, no downloads.

## Long Description (2000 chars)
Remote Command pays homage to the classic DOS artillery games (Scorched Earth, Worms, Tank Wars) while bringing them fully into the modern browser era. Every weapon, every terrain, every match is built for fast-paced tactical fun.

**Features:**
- 20+ unique weapons: Missiles, Nuclear Bombs, MIRVs, Cluster Bombs, Homing Shells, Lasers, Railguns, Napalm, Torpedoes, Underwater Mines, Airstrikes, and more
- 9 themed environments: Forest, Desert, Canyon, Arctic, Ocean (underwater physics!), Cave, Moon, Mars, Futuristic
- Fully destructible terrain — every explosion carves a crater
- Realistic physics: Gravity, wind (5 modes from calm to chaotic), projectile trajectories
- 3 AI difficulty levels with smart weapon selection and wind compensation
- 2-8 players (mix of humans and AI)
- Multiple game modes: Classic, Quick Battle, Chaos, Target Practice, Teams (2x4), Realtime
- Power-up crates: Health, ammo, fuel, shields
- Drive mode: Reposition your tank tactically between shots
- Mobile-optimized with touch controls, joystick, and haptic feedback
- 24 sound effects, 3 music tracks
- Day/night cycle with dynamic lighting
- Water physics: Torpedoes, depth charges, sonar pulses on ocean maps

**Controls (Desktop):**
- Arrow Keys / WASD — angle & power
- Q/E — cycle weapons
- Space / Enter — fire
- P — pause

**Controls (Mobile):**
- Joystick for movement (drive mode)
- Angle dial + power slider
- Tap weapon icon to switch, tap FIRE to launch

Free to play, no ads on gameplay (only optional rewarded ads for in-game bonuses). No downloads, no signup, instant play.
